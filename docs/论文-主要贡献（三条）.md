# 论文第一部分末尾「主要贡献」三条（中英）

## 中文版（可直接或稍作修改使用）

本文的主要贡献如下：

**（1）无标注辅助事件推断与联合训练。** 在 Time-LLM 骨干之上增加与主任务共享表征的辅助分类头，用于推断「本预测窗口是否存在发运」等离散事件状态；辅助标签完全由数据推导（未来段内任一时间任一道非零则记为有发运），无需人工标注。通过联合优化目标 L_total = L_main + λ·L_aux，在提升数值预测的同时使同一隐表征具备事件级可解释性，并可选采用零膨胀加权损失与多通道 MASE 以适配零膨胀、多通道场景下的训练稳定性与预测质量。本实验为多通道同时预测（四通道联合输入与输出、共享表征），未显式建模通道间协同或依赖关系。

**（2）事件驱动的可解释输出与零幻觉解码。** 在推理阶段将数值预测与辅助判定统一为可操作语义：由辅助头给出发运/空闲二分类及置信度，由预测序列统计「有发运天数」并填入模板；同时基于历史与预测序列的规则推演，自动生成趋势、发运连续性、品种间关系及预测期总量与主导品种等业务描述，最终通过固定模板生成 Dispatch Log 等确定性文本。语义输出全程不经过大模型生成，避免幻觉，形成「数值预测 → 事件判定 → 业务语义」的闭环，增强调度决策中的可解释性与可用性。

**（3）完整实验框架与可复现基准。** 在铁路货运四类货品（铁矿石、铁矿砂、铁矿粉、铁精矿粉）数据上建立了从领域 prompt、联合训练与损失设计、到评估与可解释性分析的完整流程，并与多种基线（如 XGBoost、LSTM、ARIMA、Prophet 等）在相同划分与指标下对比；同时提供重编程层文本原型与业务词集的权重视图及时间推演语义输出，为「为何如此预测」与事件级结论提供可解释性支撑，为后续零膨胀多通道时序与领域可解释性研究提供可复现基准。

## 英文版（Contributions）

The main contributions of this paper are summarized as follows:

**（1）Label-free auxiliary event deduction and joint training.** We extend the Time-LLM backbone with an auxiliary classification head that shares the same latent representation as the main forecasting task and infers discrete event states such as “whether dispatch exists in the current forecast window.” Auxiliary labels are derived entirely from data (dispatch if any time step and any channel is non-zero in the future window), requiring no human annotation. By jointly optimizing L_total = L_main + λ·L_aux, we improve both numeric forecasting and event-level interpretability of the shared representation. We further support zero-inflated weighted loss and multi-channel MASE for training stability and forecasting quality in zero-inflated, multi-channel settings. The setup is multi-channel joint forecasting (four channels, shared representation) without explicitly modeling cross-channel dependencies or synergy.

**（2）Event-driven interpretable output and zero-hallucination decoding.** At inference, we unify numeric predictions and auxiliary decisions into actionable semantics: the auxiliary head yields a binary dispatch/idle label and confidence; the predicted series is used to count “active days” and fill templates; and a rule-based time-evolution module over history and forecast produces trend, transport continuity, inter-variety relations, and total volume and dominant varieties. All semantic text is produced via fixed templates, without LLM-generated language, thus avoiding hallucination. This yields a “numeric forecast → event deduction → business semantics” pipeline and improves interpretability and usability for scheduling decisions.

**（3）Full experimental framework and reproducible benchmark.** We establish an end-to-end pipeline on railway freight data over four commodity types (iron ore, iron ore sand, iron ore fines, iron concentrate), including domain prompts, joint training and loss design, evaluation, and interpretability (semantic outputs and prototype–word visualizations). We compare against multiple baselines (e.g., XGBoost, LSTM, ARIMA, Prophet) under the same data split and metrics, and provide reprogramming-layer prototype–word weight views and time-evolution semantic reports to support “why this forecast” and event-level conclusions. The framework serves as a reproducible benchmark for zero-inflated multi-channel forecasting and domain interpretability.

## 使用说明

- **（1）** 强调**方法创新**：无标注辅助头、数据推导伪标签、联合损失 L_total = L_main + λ·L_aux；可选带一句零膨胀/MASE 适配。
- **（2）** 强调**可解释与落地**：辅助判定 + 有发运天数 + 规则时间推演 + 模板化 Dispatch Log，零幻觉。
- **（3）** 强调**实验与基准**：完整流程、四类货品、多基线对比、原型–词集可视化与语义输出、可复现性。
- 若篇幅紧张，可在（1）中略写「零膨胀/MASE」，在（3）中略写「多种基线」或「权重视图」，保留「完整流程 + 可复现基准」即可。
- 若期刊偏重方法，可将（1）再拆为「联合训练与无标注辅助」与「损失与稳定性」两句，或把损失一句并入（3）的实验设定。
